Privacy Policy

Courssak Driver

Last Updated: October 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Courssak Driver ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our driver mobile application.

By using the Courssak Driver app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register and use our driver app, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name and contact information (phone number, email address)
  • Profile picture and driver photo
  • Driver's license information and vehicle registration details
  • Vehicle information (make, model, year, license plate, color)
  • Banking and payment information for earnings deposits
  • Background check results (as permitted by law)
  • Trip history, earnings, and performance metrics
  • Location data (GPS coordinates for navigation and ride tracking)

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and app usage:

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent, actions taken)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, app crashes)
  • Driving behavior data (speed, acceleration, braking patterns)

2.3 Location Information

We collect continuous location data when you are online and available for rides, during active trips, and for navigation purposes. This data is essential for matching you with passengers, providing turn-by-turn directions, and ensuring safety. You can control location permissions through your device settings, but disabling location services will prevent you from accepting ride requests.

2.4 Financial Information

We collect information necessary to process payments to you, including bank account details, tax identification numbers, and transaction history. This information is processed securely through our payment service providers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity and approve your driver application
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our ride-sharing platform
  • To match you with passengers and facilitate ride connections
  • To process your earnings and make payments
  • To provide navigation and route optimization
  • To monitor and improve driver safety and service quality
  • To send you ride requests, notifications, and important updates
  • To provide customer and driver support
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the driver experience
  • To ensure platform safety and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Passengers

When you accept a ride request, we share necessary information with passengers including your name, photo, vehicle details, license plate, and real-time location to help them identify you and track the ride.

4.2 With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, including payment processors, background check providers, mapping services, and customer support tools.

4.3 For Safety and Legal Purposes

We may disclose your information to law enforcement, government authorities, or other parties when required by law or to protect the safety and security of our users.

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • End-to-end encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure storage of financial and identification information
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-DSS compliant providers
  • Multi-factor authentication options

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you maintain an active driver account and for a reasonable period thereafter as necessary to comply with legal obligations (such as tax and employment records), resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Trip data and earnings history may be retained for several years for legal and business purposes.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications
  • Location: Control location permissions (note: this will affect your ability to accept rides)
  • Account Deactivation: Deactivate your driver account through the app settings
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

8. Driver-Specific Considerations

8.1 Background Checks

As part of the driver onboarding process, we conduct background checks. The results are used solely to determine eligibility to drive on our platform and are handled in accordance with applicable laws.

8.2 Earnings and Tax Information

We collect and report your earnings information as required by tax laws. You are responsible for reporting your earnings and paying applicable taxes.

8.3 Ratings and Feedback

Passengers can rate and provide feedback on your service. This information is used to maintain service quality standards and may affect your ability to continue using the platform.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
